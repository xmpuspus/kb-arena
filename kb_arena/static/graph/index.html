About the graph

The knowledge graph is built by the LLM entity extractor during kb-arena build-graph. Entities and relationships are extracted using a universal schema — Topics, Components, Processes, Configs, and Constraints, connected by DEPENDS_ON, CONTAINS, CONNECTS_TO, TRIGGERS, CONFIGURES, ALTERNATIVE_TO, and EXTENDS relationships. The graph is stored in Neo4j and queried with Cypher templates matched to question intent.
